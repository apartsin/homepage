Research

Home / Research

My research focuses on robust applied AI: systems that remain useful under noisy, data-limited, and operationally constrained real-world conditions. Each project begins with a concrete problem and is expected to produce usable artifacts, including code, data, and empirical evidence. I publish this work and involve graduate students as collaborators, with results feeding back into courses, supervision, and new project directions.
