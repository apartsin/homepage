Research Focus

Home / Research / Research Focus

My research focuses on applied AI for robust perception across vision, voice, signal, and text; spec-driven generative systems; and distributed agentic intelligence.

Robust Vision AI

Visual understanding under adverse viewing conditions, motion, degradation, and uncertainty. Recent work: license plate recoverability under extreme angles and panoramic incident summaries from body-worn footage.

Spec-Driven GenAI

Controllable generation of molecules, text, and images under target constraints. Active projects: domain-gated latent diffusion for energetic materials, interactive diffusion steering, and speculative code generation.

Robust Voice AI

Speech understanding under noise, degradation, stress, and transmission errors. Recent work: critical information extraction from maritime distress communications and robust motion compensation for forensic egocentric video analysis.

Robust Signal AI

From extreme noise suppression to anomaly detection in big data. Past work includes early detection of engine anomalies for vehicle health management (2022) and time-of-flight estimation under outliers (2014).

Robust Text AI

LLMs and agentic AI for active information gathering and forecasting under ambiguity and noise. Active projects: implicit entity recognition in reminiscence narratives and evidence matrix reasoning for geopolitical forecasting. Recent paper: LLM diagnostic questioning benchmark.

Distributed AI

Coordination, evaluation, and reasoning across multiple agents under limited information. Active projects: zero-knowledge swarm coordination, self-evaluating model ensembles, and collaborative reasoning framework.
