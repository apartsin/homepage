Home / Building / Consulting

I collaborate with companies and research groups on applied AI projects that bridge industry practice and academic research. These engagements draw on three decades of building systems and the Innovation-First Learning approach I developed through that experience.

Problem Framing & Business-Technology Alignment

AI creates value only when the right problem is being solved and the solution fits both business priorities and technical reality. That alignment is often difficult because strategy, product, and AI capability are rarely expressed in the same language. I help bring those perspectives together, sharpen the scope, and translate ambition into clear, actionable direction. The result is stronger decision-making, less wasted effort, and AI initiatives that are positioned to deliver real business value.

Technology & Scientific Blueprint

Promising AI products need more than a good idea. They need a clear technical and methodological foundation that can support credible execution. I help turn product goals into a focused blueprint covering models, architecture, evaluation criteria, data requirements, and implementation priorities. Drawing on experience across both innovation-driven environments and complex organizations, this approach helps reduce technical risk, avoid fragile early decisions, and create a more reliable path from concept to delivery.

Rapid Prototyping & Demo

AI-assisted development now makes it possible to test ideas far more quickly, but speed alone is not enough. Early prototypes need to answer the right questions, reveal technical feasibility, and explore product alternatives without creating false confidence. I design and build focused PoCs and demos that are guided by both product intent and technical depth, so rapid experimentation produces meaningful insight. This makes it easier to validate direction, reduce uncertainty, and support smarter product and investment decisions.

Industry-Academia Research

Joint research projects connecting company problems with graduate-level research capacity, including M.Sc. student supervision on company-sponsored topics, publication-ready research with practical deliverables, and grant proposal support.
