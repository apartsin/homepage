Selected milestones and recognized outcomes from the original innovation-and-entrepreneurship track.

Amdocs: Model-Based Capacity Planning

I designed a statistical model used to predict load on large distributed billing systems. It supported more accurate sizing of system installations and ultimately saved the company $100 million in unnecessary hardware costs.

Impact: $100M savings

Citibank: Financial Data Science Platform

While at Citibank TLV lab, I proposed creating a corporate platform for sharing and collaborating on data science insights across the organization. The project was awarded a corporate LabLink grant to support its development.

Recognition: LabLink grant

Motorola Solutions: AI for Public Safety

I led the research team at the first AI lab at Motorola Solutions' Innovation Center, designed the lab’s roadmap, and proposed multiple initiatives across video, text, and speech use cases. I also led several industry-academia collaboration projects, securing grants from the Israel Innovation Authority (IIA) to support their development.

Recognition: IIA grants

SafeRide: AI for Automotive Telematics

I established a data science team and helped define a big-data-driven AI product for no-code creation of onboard models that monitor signal anomalies. The product was recognized as Automotive AI Product of the Year. The company was acquired by Questar Automotive.

Recognition: AI Product of the Year
