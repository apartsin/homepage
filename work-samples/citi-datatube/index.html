Citi DataTube

Enterprise Data Insight Platform · Project Brief

A web portal for publishing, discovering, and collaborating on data insights and visualizations across the enterprise. DataTube brings the simplicity of discovering and sharing video content to corporate data analytics.

Problems Addressed

  1. Data insights buried in email attachments and spreadsheets
  2. Knowledge lost in scattered discussion threads
  3. Limited collaboration across teams and departments
  4. High cost of ad-hoc report generation and licensing
  5. Months-long time-to-value for data dissemination

Key Capabilities

Publish

Upload datasets, connect to warehouses and streams, auto-generate visuals.

Discover

Search, browse, and get personalized recommendations by role.

Collaborate

Discuss, annotate, and share insights with preserved context.

Combine

Mix-and-match datasets into composite dashboards.

Govern

Enterprise access control, SSO, and compliance.

Core Technologies
