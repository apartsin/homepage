Building Temporal AI with Sequential Intelligence and Reinforcement Learning

A code-first course on building temporal intelligence systems for forecasting, reinforcement learning, and world models. Part of the Hands-on AI Science series, designed around Innovation-First Learning principles.

Temporal Intelligence

A unified approach to time series modeling, causal reasoning, anomaly detection, and reinforcement learning for systems that operate reliably over time.

World Models

Learned representations of environment dynamics that enable imagination, counterfactual reasoning, and long-horizon prediction without direct interaction.

Guided Projects

Students begin projects early and enrich them with new concepts throughout the course, with iterative in-class presentations and feedback.
