Building Scalable AI with Big Data and Distributed Intelligence

Home / Teaching / Course Syllabi / Building Scalable AI with Big Data and Distributed Intelligence

A code-first course on building scalable data systems with distributed algorithms. Part of the Hands-on AI Science series, designed around Innovation-First Learning principles.

Big Data Algorithms

Distributed computing models that decompose tasks such as sorting, aggregation, and joins into parallel subtasks executed across multiple machines.

Data-Intensive Systems

Architectures designed to store, process, and analyze large volumes of data that cannot be handled by a single computer.

Guided Projects

Students begin projects early and enrich them with new concepts throughout the course, with iterative in-class presentations and feedback.
