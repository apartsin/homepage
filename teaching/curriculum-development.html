This section summarizes core curriculum initiatives that align computer science education with current AI practice and graduate readiness.

CS Degree Curriculum Design

While leading the Software Engineering track at HIT School of Computer Science, I prepared a proposal for an updated CS degree curriculum centered on Innovation-First Learning principles.

CS Graduate Readiness Profile

I established and led an industry advisory board of hiring professionals and alumni to define clear readiness profiles for key graduate roles, including AI engineer and full-stack developer tracks.

Innovation-First Learning

Innovation-First Learning addresses the gap between traditional curricula and real AI practice by combining theory, modern tools, and substantial projects from the start of the learning process.
